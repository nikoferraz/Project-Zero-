Composting is a good idea and here is why ...

Not only does compost replenish the soil, it also prevents diseases.

Compost is your way to provide energy to your soil. Each year, garden plants eat out the organic material of the soil. Compost replenishes the organic material (up to 10% of the soil for optimum growth) to provide food for your plants.


Your soil will be healthier, and your plants will grow better. Compost also helps improve aeration, water retention, lessens disease. Worm compost is full of microbial life, humic acids, and hormones that prevent disease, lead to maximum growth, and retains more water. (Compost Fundamentals: Benefits & Uses - benefits of compost)
