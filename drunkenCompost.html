We make dirt. Cheap.

Sustainably sourced soil, customized for your needs.

Colorado breweries have hundreds of thousands of pounds of spent grain waste daily from brewing beer. Colorado soils are challenged by erosion, poor microbial availability, poor water retention and pesticide use. Drunken Worm Compost symbiotically solves both of these problems by taking spent grain waste from breweries to create the best compost for growers. We connect the breweries with the gardener through innovative marketing, building a market for the brewery and the compost.
